About ElectroNinja

Empowering RGPV ECE Warriors

Our Mission

To transform every RGPV Electronics & Communication Engineering student into a confident, exam-crushing ElectroNinja through comprehensive PYQ coverage, AI-powered solutions, and a warrior mindset.

We believe that with the right resources and practice, every student can master circuits, ace their exams, and build a strong foundation for their engineering career.

The ElectroNinja Story

🎯

The Problem

RGPV ECE students were struggling to find organized, unit-wise previous year questions. Scattered PDFs, incomplete resources, and no clear roadmap to exam preparation. Hours wasted searching for the right questions. Confusion about what to practice. Anxiety before exams.

💡

The Vision

What if there was a platform that organized ALL PYQs by semester, subject, and unit? What if AI could provide instant solutions to any ECE question? What if preparing for exams felt like training to become a ninja - focused, powerful, and exciting?

⚡

The Solution

ElectroNinja was born. A dark, energetic platform where ECE students become warriors. Every PYQ from 2022-2025, organized by unit. AI-powered instant solutions. A ninja-tech aesthetic that makes studying actually exciting. And the best part? Completely free, forever.

🚀

The Future

Today, ElectroNinja serves ECE Sem 3 & 4 students with comprehensive resources. Tomorrow, we'll expand to all semesters, add progress tracking, build a community of ninja warriors, and eventually serve all RGPV engineering branches. Our goal: Every RGPV student becomes a ninja in their field.
